Virtu

Virtu is a decentralized cryptocurrency tailored for the Virtual Reality (VR) ecosystem. Designed to provide seamless transactions within VR applications, Virtu empowers users to purchase virtual goods, tip creators, and incentivize developments in the VR realm.




Roadmap

- Virtu Whitepaper Release: A comprehensive overview of Virtu's technology, goals, and vision. - Initial Wallet Development: User-friendly VR-compatible wallet for storing and transacting Virtu.

- Virtu Testnet Launch: Testing the network's stability and security. - VR App SDK Integration: Tools for VR developers to integrate Virtu payments into their applications.

- Expansion to AR (Augmented Reality): Opening doors to the AR ecosystem. - Enhanced Security Protocols: Implementing state-of-the-art security measures to ensure safe transactions.

- Virtu Mainnet Launch: Official public release of the Virtu network. - Partnership Announcements: Collaboration with key VR platforms and developers.

- Virtual Marketplace Integration: Use Virtu to buy and sell virtual goods and services. - VR Gaming Integration: Partner with VR game developers to allow in-game purchases with Virtu.

Creator Incentive Program: Reward VR content creators for driving the adoption of Virtu. - Virtu Debit Card Launch: Spend Virtu in the real world.
